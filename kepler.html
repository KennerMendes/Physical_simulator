Kepler's second law

This law says that the line between a planet and your sun sweeps equal areas in equal time intervals. Which means (among other things) that the closer the planet is to the sun, faster it is.

Realize the velocity variation is a consequence of the energy conservation law. If planet is close to the sun your potential energy is lower, so kinetic energy needs to be higher, if it's far, potential energy is higher, kinetic energy needs to be lower.
